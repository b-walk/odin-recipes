Restaurant-Style Pickle

Description

Everybody loves a good, crunchy pickle. We can all relate to the age-old stereotype of pulling our pocket-pickles out and enjoying a nice pickley snack during our daily commute. This recipe is an ode to our timeless companion: the pickle.

Ingredients

  • Pickle
  • Spaghetti
  • Living human

Instructions

  1. Refrigerate your pickle
  2. Imprison a living human
  3. Feed your subject spaghetti to Italianize their liver
  4. Wait one hour
  5. Extract the human subject's liver
  6. Place liver in boiling water for 11 minutes, then remove promptly
  7. Get your pickle from the fridge
  8. Enjoy!
